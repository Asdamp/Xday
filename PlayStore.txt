Have you ever wondered to calculate how many days are left to your birthday?
Or how many days did you live in your entire life?
Or how many minutes do you have before your holyday end?
Xday answers all your questions.
Set a date, set an hour and Xday will calculate for you the time left or passed collecting all your preferred date in one single screen, constantly updated.
Calculating days left to an event will not be a problem anymore and you can check them using the widget included in the app.
